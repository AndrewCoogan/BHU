What is BHU?

BHU stands for Bang For Buck Housing Upgade, but I thought the moniker BHU was cuter. BHU gives Homeowners insight into how remodeling might impact the value of their house. Labor and materials are expensive, both monetarily and temporally. A homeowner deserves to know how much that might result if they choose to move. That is where BHU steps in--based on your address, BHU loads a custom regional price model and allows you to add a bedroom, or take away a half bathroom and make it full. The model used is a deep neural network with about 20 features including basic stats like square footage and number of beds, but also neighborhoods, how accessible the house is to local amenities.
